## What Klarix Does

Done-for-you competitive intelligence for B2B SaaS, recruiting, FinTech, VC, and agencies. Prospect dossiers, SWOT analyses, battle cards, one-pagers, outreach sequences, and market overviews delivered in 3–7 days — no platform to learn, no analyst to hire.

Live at [klarix.ai](https://klarix.ai). Built solo across three distinct engineering surfaces.

## 1 · Marketing site — `klarix.ai`

Next.js 14 App Router site engineered end-to-end for SEO, conversion, and Core Web Vitals.

- **99/100 Mobile PageSpeed, 100/100 Desktop.** Shipped a performance pass that removed `framer-motion` entirely, replaced it with a 40-line `IntersectionObserver`-based `FadeIn`, lazy-loaded GA/Clarity, and added immutable cache headers. **LCP dropped from 5.0s → under 2s; TBT from 1,400ms → ~100ms.**
- **SEO foundation.** Programmatic sitemap and 8 JSON-LD schema types (Organization, WebSite, Service, OfferCatalog, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Article, Offer) composed across nested App Router layouts — so "CI for SaaS" and "CI for recruiting" rank as distinct entities.
- **Conversion infrastructure.** Global click auto-tracker fires 11 GA4 events across every CTA without per-component instrumentation. Google Consent Mode v2 with cookie banner. LinkedIn Insight Tag for retargeting. Exit-intent modal on high-intent pages. Route + global error boundaries reporting to GA4.
- **16 pages** including 4 competitor comparison pages (vs Klue, Crayon, Apollo, ZoomInfo, Aqute), 2 industry pillars, service pillar, pricing, samples, blog, and a polished 404 with a popular-pages grid. First Load JS: 88.2 kB.

## 2 · Deliverable pipeline — Python + multi-provider AI

A config-driven agentic pipeline that turns a company list into six sales-ready artifacts per prospect. Each research stage has different cost, latency, and quality tradeoffs — a single model can't handle all of them efficiently, so providers are assigned by stage.

| Stage | Provider | Reason |
|-------|----------|--------|
| ICP scoring | MiniMax M2.7 Highspeed | Low cost, 950 RPM throughput |
| Research synthesis | Gemini 3.1 Pro | Long context, multi-source summarization |
| Outreach copy | Claude 4.5 Opus | Highest written-deliverable quality |
| Contact collection | Groq Compound + Llama 70B | Sub-second structured extraction |
| Deep research | Tavily | Real-time web retrieval |
| Contact enrichment | Apollo API | Verified email + firmographics |

**Reliability at 128 concurrent threads:**

- **3-tier failover** (MiniMax → OpenRouter → Groq), activating automatically on rate-limit or API error.
- **Append-under-lock JSONL checkpointing** — every completed record flushes to disk; crashed runs resume from the last checkpoint, not from scratch.
- **Per-provider rate-limit budgets** enforced at the thread-pool level, preventing cascading failures when one provider degrades.

**Six deliverables per company** (~400 artifacts for a 100-company engagement): prospect dossiers, SWOT analyses, competitor battle cards, executive one-pagers, outreach sequences (email + LinkedIn + voicemail), and pricing intelligence.

## 3 · Outreach engine — 1,240-contact personalization at scale

End-to-end contact-to-campaign pipeline built on top of the deliverable output.

- **Apollo enrichment:** 432 companies → 1,240 decision-maker contacts, enriched with funding, headcount, tenure, and tech stack.
- **Role-archetype classification:** each contact tagged `champion`, `economic_buyer`, `influencer`, `gatekeeper`, or `practitioner` with a 1–9 fit score.
- **Per-contact personalization:** 51-column enriched record including LinkedIn connection message, email subject + body, 3 follow-ups, voicemail script, and LinkedIn DM draft — each generated from company-specific sales intel (funding events, achievements, surprise facts, CI angle) pulled per row.
- **Humanness review pass:** AI-driven tone and spam-filter audit before send.
- **Result: 618 sendable-today contacts** (fit ≥ 7, has email, buyer / champion / influencer) across 323 companies, queued for Instantly upload.

## Selected engineering highlights

- **Removed `framer-motion` programmatically** across 8 files via a 120-line PowerShell refactor with balanced-brace AST-aware const-declaration stripping — eliminated ~50 kB bundle and cut TBT by 93%.
- **Google Consent Mode v2** with `beforeInteractive` default-deny, `localStorage`-backed consent state, a `CustomEvent`-based consent bus, and granular script gating per vendor — GA4 and Clarity load only after opt-in, with zero cold-start flicker.
- **Global click auto-tracker** hooks `document` in the capture phase, inspects `href` and `data-*` attributes at bubble time, and routes 11 distinct GA4 events — obviating per-component instrumentation while still discriminating pricing / compare / sample / service intents.
- **Per-route schema orchestration** via nested App Router layouts — each pillar page's `layout.tsx` composes breadcrumb JSON-LD with its own Service / Article schema while the root layout emits cross-site Organization / FAQPage.
